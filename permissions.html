应用权限说明

2023-09-26

设备信息权限
收集设备信息，用于识别账号异常状态，了解产品适配性，维护基础功能的正常运行，实现本游戏的问题排查。
 
存储权限
用于使应用程序在运行时对游戏资源进行必要的缓存和读取。
 
地理位置权限
获取地理位置信息，用于寻找附近的游戏玩家，以便与附近的玩家匹配、组队等。
 
相机权限
iOS 设备还可能获取相册权限、照片权限收集图片信息（个人信息），用以提供空间图像展示个性化服务。
 
麦克风权限
收集语音信息（个人信息），用以提供语音形式的社交功能服务。
 

Application Permissions Description

2023-09-26

Device information permissions
Collect device information to identify abnormal account status, understand product adaptability, maintain the normal operation of basic functions, and troubleshoot problems in this game.
 
Storage permissions
Used to enable the application to perform necessary caching and reading of game resources during runtime.
 
Geolocation permissions
Obtain geographical location information to find nearby game players to match, form teams with nearby players, etc.
 
Camera permissions
iOS devices may also obtain album permissions and photo permissions to collect picture information (personal information) to provide personalized spatial image display services.
 
Microphone permissions
Collect voice information (personal information) to provide social function services in the form of voice.
